Definition · 02
Understanding the Stage

What is Adolescence?

Adolescence is the transitional stage of physical, cognitive, emotional and social growth between childhood and adulthood — typically spanning ages 12 to 18. It is marked by puberty, identity formation, and the search for independence.

15–16

17–18

Example: A 13-year-old experiencing a sudden growth spurt while also questioning family rules for the first time — a classic sign of early adolescence.

Physical Growth · 03
Body Changes

Physical Development

Boys

Broadening shoulders, voice deepening, facial & body hair growth, increased muscle mass.

Girls

Widening of hips, breast development, onset of menstruation, growth spurt occurs earlier.

Both

Rapid height increase, skin changes (acne), increased sweat & oil glands, hormonal surges.
